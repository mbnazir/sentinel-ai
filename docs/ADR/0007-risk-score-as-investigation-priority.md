# ADR-0007: Risk score represents investigation priority

## Status

Accepted

## Context

The platform must avoid making unsupported legal conclusions. Rule findings and scores should help investigators prioritize cases.

## Decision

Use risk score as an investigation priority indicator, not a declaration of fraud.

## Consequences

- UI and reports should use terms like integrity risk, suspicious, review, and critical.
- Human investigation remains required.
- AI summaries must explain evidence, not accuse.
