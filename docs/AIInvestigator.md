# AI Investigator / Evidence Narrative Engine

Milestone 9 introduces the AI narrative layer.

## Principle

AI does not detect fraud and does not make accusations.

AI summarizes deterministic evidence generated by:

- Timeline Engine
- Activity Matching Engine
- Rule Engine
- Risk Scoring Engine
- Behavior Analytics
- Investigation Case Management

## Provider abstraction

Milestone 9 introduces:

- `BaseAIProvider`
- `MockAIProvider`
- `OpenAIProvider` scaffold

Future providers:

- Azure OpenAI
- Gemini
- Claude
- Ollama
- LM Studio

## Safety rules

Prompts instruct the model to:

- avoid accusations
- avoid legal conclusions
- summarize only provided evidence
- separate findings, evidence, next steps, and limitations
- use neutral investigation language

## Output

`InvestigationNarrative` contains:

- executive summary
- key findings
- evidence summary
- recommended next steps
- limitations
- raw model output
