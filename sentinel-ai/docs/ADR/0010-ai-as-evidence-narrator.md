# ADR-0010: AI as evidence narrator, not decision maker

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Workforce integrity investigations are sensitive. AI-generated accusations would create legal, HR, and trust risks.

## Decision

AI will only summarize deterministic evidence and provide review recommendations. It will not determine fraud.

## Consequences

- Deterministic rules remain the source of truth.
- AI outputs must include limitations.
- Prompts must use neutral language.
- Human review remains mandatory.
